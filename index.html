Why Personalize?

Instead of sending a generic email that lands in the trash, a personalized email feels more authentic by showing how your product or service directly addresses their specific needs.

However personalized emails take time, but Voltmailer solves this by scraping website data to automate personalization. Just provide the leads, and we handle the rest.

Here’s how it works!

Upload / Create a lead list

  • Quickly upload a csv file
  • Enter a domain and we’ll find the associated emails
  • Add leads by inputting emails,websites and prospect names
Get Started

Enter Your Pitch

  • Enter your business Pitch
  • Keep it concise with a clear CTA
  • We try and achieve your CTA in the emails we send for you

Review and send them off

  • We will allow you to view every email before you send it through our manual send
  • Or you can automatically send of your emails and wait for a reply from your prospects
